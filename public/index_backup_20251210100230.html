About

Hey, I’m Karthikaa Kannan — a 17-year-old high schooler with a billion-dollar mindset and a relentless passion for creating, leading, and inspiring.

I’m the Founder/CEO of The Narrative Identity (TNI) — a visionary branding firm helping brands express their stories with authenticity. What started as a solo vision is now a team of 20+ skilled students united by craft and excellence.

Beyond brands, I mentor over 50 students in storytelling, scriptwriting, and freelancing — guiding them to design lives of independence. Ambition is a skill learned through consistency.

I’m building NEXTEP, a student-first platform empowering youth to learn, earn, and grow. With a community of 500+ students across India, we are moving toward new-age learning.

Success isn’t about money; it’s about meaning. Legacy is built in the change you create for others. Let’s learn together and grow together.
